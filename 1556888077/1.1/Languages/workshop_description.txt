[h1]Description :[/h1]
[1.0] DE Surgeries

The more the surgery difficulty increase, the more the Quality/Cleanliness of the hospital bed/room and the MedicalÅ@skill value will impact chances of success


[h1]Easy surgeries:[/h1]
You can choose to quickly treat those injuries instead of waiting for them to slowly heal (and maybe cause infections), but it will cost you more medical resources and medicine skills. Colonist who can do this difficulty surgery are called doctors or healers.

[b]Subject of surgery:[/b]
Burn, Crush, Crack, Cut, Surgical cut, Scratch, Bite, Stab, Gunshot, Shredded, Bruise and Frostbite.

[b]Work Amount[/b] : 2,000.
[b]Medicine Requires[/b] : 4 units of medicine (herbal medicine can be used).
[b]Skill Requires[/b] : level 8 or higher in Medical.


[h1]Normal surgeries:[/h1]
Colonist who can perform this difficulty surgery are called senior doctor or healer head (tribe).

[b]Subject of surgery:[/b]
Mandible, Finger, Toe, Clavicles, Ear, Nose and Hearing loss.

[b]Work Amount[/b] : 3,000.
[b]Medicine Requires[/b] : 4 units of medicine (herbal medicine cannot be used).
[b]Skill Requires[/b] : level 12 or higher in Medical.

[b]Resource Stuff Requires :[/b]
 Mandible and clavicles surgeries requires 15 Plasteel.


[h1]Hard surgeries:[/h1]
[b]=== IMPORTANT ===[/b]
On failure there is 15% chance to kill the patient.

You can try to heal Muscle Parasites and Gut Worms in one operation (instead of waiting for the 5 treatments in Vanilla) but it will cost you 10 units of medicine in one time (x2 than nomal version). Also be aware of the risk of lethal failures (if the operation fail then there is a 20% chance of killing the patient). Colonist who succeed in this difficulty surgery are called honorary doctors or legendary healers (tribes).

[b]Subject of surgery:[/b]
Asthma, Heart artery blockage, Cataract, Ribcage, Sternum, Radius, Humerus, Femur, Tibia, Muscle Parasites and Gut Worms, infection.

[b]Work Amount[/b] : 4,500.
[b]Medicine Requires[/b] : 6 units of medicine (herbal medicine cannot be used).
[b]Skill Requires[/b] : level 15 or higher in Medical.

[b]Resource Stuff Requires :[/b]
 Sternum repair also requires 30 Plasteel.
 Ribcage repair also requires 12 Plasteel (Equivalent to twelve ribs).
 Muscle Parasites and Gut Worms treatment requires 10 units of medicine
 Cure wound infection requires 5 units of Penoxycyline.


[h1]VeryHard surgeries:[/h1]
[b]=== IMPORTANT ===[/b]
On failure there is 20% chance to kill the patient.

You can try to heal Muscle Parasites and Gut Worms in one operation (instead of waiting for the 5 treatments in Vanilla) but it will cost you 10 units of medicine in one time (x2 than nomal version). Also be aware of the risk of lethal failures (if the operation fail then there is a 10% chance of killing the patient). Colonist who succeed in this difficulty surgery are called honorary doctors or legendary healers (tribes).

[b]Subject of surgery:[/b]
Spine, Pelvis, Dementia, Alzheimers, Bad Back, Frail, Cirrhosis.

[b]Work Amount[/b] : 6,000.
[b]Medicine Requires[/b] : 6 units of medicine (herbal medicine cannot be used).
[b]Skill Requires[/b] : level 17 or higher in Medical.

[b]Resource Stuff Requires :[/b]
 Spine and Pelvis repair also requires 80 Plasteel.
 Cure Dementia and Cure Alzheimers also requires 6 glitterworld medicine.
 Cure/repair Bad Back requires also requires 80 Plasteel and 6 medicine.
 Cure Frail requires also requires 120 Plasteel and 6 medicine.
 Cure Cirrhosis requires also requires 6 glitterworld medicine.
